About My School

Kenyerere Junior Academy was founded in 1974 with the aim and vision to provide education to the community of Kenyerere which was lacking in quality education.


In 2024 we celebrated our 50 years anniversary by refurbishing, repainting and rebranding of the school.


We aim to provide first class education from Playgroup all the way to Grade 6 CBC Curriculum at an affordable cost while providing the best teachers and facilities.
